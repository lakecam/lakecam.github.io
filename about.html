It’s me, Lake!

I was born and raised on the Malaysian side of tropical Borneo island, a place filled with strong and vibrant colours, from the hornbills that would occasionally snatch my lunch from the balcony to the fresh and colourful flora that paraded the lush rainforest trees next to my house. This has influenced my sense of visual design through my love of bold and adventurous colours, and my need to add a touch of vibrancy in everything that I make.

While some may find my plan of working in user experience design and finishing a university credential in interaction design ‘a bit much’, I enjoy learning an thinking and learning a broad array of principles and then immediately applying them into my job, it’s been rewarding and a journey that i’m excited to continue.
